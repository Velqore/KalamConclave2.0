SCIENCE SLAM RULEBOOK
Whispers of Hope, Suffering, and Survival

1. About the Event
The Science Slam is an interdisciplinary competition that blends science with creative expression. It provides a platform for participants to explore global war situations through poetry, rap, and open mic performances. The event highlights the role of science and technology in times of conflict, emphasizing themes of resilience, innovation, survival, and hope.

2. Theme
“Whispers of Hope, Suffering, and Survival”
Participants are encouraged to creatively interpret the impact of ongoing conflicts across the world while maintaining scientific relevance and humanitarian sensitivity.
Suggested Sub-Themes
Medical innovations and healthcare in war zones
Environmental consequences of warfare
Nuclear science and ethical dilemmas
Artificial intelligence and cyber warfare
Space and satellite technologies in defence
Refugee crises and humanitarian technologies
Psychological trauma and rehabilitation
Science for peace and global recovery

3. Nature of the Competition
This is one unified competition in which participants may present their ideas using any one of the following formats:
Poetry Slam
Open Mic Performance
Science Rap
All formats will be evaluated together under a single judging system, and winners will be selected based on overall merit.

4. Eligibility Criteria
Open to all

5. General Rules
Performances must be original and unpublished.
Content must align with the theme.
Participants may perform in English, Hindi, or bilingually.
Vulgarity, hate speech, and political propaganda are strictly prohibited.
Content must remain respectful and sensitive to global conflicts.
The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

6. Time Limit
Format
Duration

Poetry Slam / Open Mic / Science Rap
2–4 minutes

A warning bell will ring 30 seconds before the end.
Exceeding the time limit may result in penalties.

7. Performance Guidelines
Minimal props are allowed.
Performances must maintain scientific accuracy and thematic relevance.
